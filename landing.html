Introduction

The 9 people boy band from South Korea.
Debuted in 2016, formed by cube Entertainment.

Pre-Debut

Pentagon (펜타곤) is a South Korean multi-national boy band formed by Cube Entertainment in 2016.The group consists of nine members: Jinho, Hui, Hongseok, Shinwon, Yeo One, Yan An, Yuto, Kino and Wooseok. Originally composed of ten members, E'Dawn left the group and the record label on November 14, 2018. They were introduced through the Mnet survival show Pentagon Maker. Pentagon released their self-titled debut EP on October 10, 2016.

Gorilla : Debut

Pentagon made their official debut on October 10, 2016 with 10 members, including the members eliminated from Pentagon Maker, E'Dawn, Shinwon, and Yan An.[ On the same day, Pentagon released their first Korean EP [Pentagon] comprising seven tracks including the title track "Gorilla", and held their debut showcase. The EP peaked number seven on the Gaon chart and remained top 10 for two consecutive weeks in the Japan's Tower Record Kpop weekly chart.

Like This : First Self-Composed Song

On September 6,2017, the group released their fourth Korean EP [Demo_01] which includes the title track "Like This", produced by member Hui. The EP marked their first all self-composed songs since debut. On November 22, the group released their fifth Korean EP [Demo_02] which includes the title track "Runaway", the second single to have been produced by Hui. Since then, Pentagon began to produce their own music and create their own color.

Shine : The Hit

On April 2,2018, Pentagon released their sixth Korean EP [Positive] which features six tracks including title track "빛나리 (Shine)". The song eventually becoming a sleeper hit by word of mouth from listeners due to its unique melody and powerful choreography. Since its release, the lowest it placed on Melon's daily chart was in the 500s, but it slowly rose to number 186 on April 29. A month after its release, on May 1, the track rose to number 95 at Melon's real-time chart. The single peaked at number 16. "Shine" debuted at number 85 on Gaon Digital Chart and peaked at number 27. Subsequently, the company announced that the promotion period was extended by two weeks. They made their debut on Top 10 Billboard's World Digital Song Sales chart. Its music video also had 200 millions views, which had became their most viewed and most famous song of all time.

PRISM : First World Tour/Road To Kingdom

On May 2,2019, Cube entertainment announced Pentagon's first world tour concert, Prism World Tour, with 15 cities worldwide and 16 shows. In July, Pentagon announced an additional eight cities for their Prism world tour, which covered twenty-three cities worldwide. On July 22, Cube Entertainment announced that Yan An would not be able to participate in Pentagon's 2019 world tour, "Prism", due to medical reasons.
Pentagon participated in Mnet's reality television competition [Road to Kingdom], which aired from April to June in 2020.For the second round with the theme of 'My Song', Pentagon chose "Shine + Spring Snow" (빛나리+봄눈) as songs that best capture their team's story. It was the last time Pentagon would perform together before the eldest member Jinho enlisted for his mandatory military service as an active duty soldier on May 11. On June 12, the group released a new song "Basquiat" for the program's finale, and finished in third place overall.

Dr.BeBe : The First Studio Album

On February 12, Pentagon released their first full album, [Universe: The Black Hall] with the lead single "Dr. Bebe". The group continued to promote as eight as Yan An did not participate in the promotions. Their first studio album features 11 tracks that were co-written or feature lyrics by members Jinho, Hui, Wooseok, Kino, and Yuto. The title song "Dr. Bebe" is an electronic genre based on hip-hop rhythm. The song contains four themes 'Love, Fall, Hurt and Crazy' it is talking about repeated 'bridle of love' that everyone falls in love with, breaks up, get hurts, goes crazy and loves again.

Daisy : The First Music Show Win

On October 12,2020, Pentagon released their tenth mini album [We:th], as an eight-member group without member Jinho, who was completing his mandatory military service. The title track, "Daisy", is an alternative rock song with a trendy yet intense sound, produced by Pentagon members Hui and Wooseok, and composer Nathan. The album consists of six songs, including "I'm Here", a self-composed solo song by Jinho. [We:th] became their highest selling album by selling 64,045 physical copies on the first week of release. On October 20, the group earned their first music show win after four years with "Daisy" on SBS MTV's The Show.

In:vite U : The Legend Never Stops

On January 24,2022, Pentagon will release their twelfth mini album [In:vite U] with the lead single "Feelin' Like", with eight members including Jinho who had already discharged. We will continue to follow Pentagon's step and give them a second win on music shows!
